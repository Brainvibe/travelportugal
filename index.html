Recommendations

Our recommended attractions

Belém Tower

Landmark medieval defensive tower

Is a 16th-century fortification located in Lisbon that served as a point of embarkation and disembarkation for Portuguese explorers and as a ceremonial gateway to Lisbon

National Palace of Pena

Hilltop Romanticist palace in parkland

A major expressions of 19th-century Romanticism in the world. UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the Seven Wonders of Portugal.

Jerónimos Monastery

World heritage listed Gothic monastery

Former monastery of the Order of Saint Jerome near the Tagus river in the parish of Belém.

Parque Natural da Arrábida

Protected natural park on the coast

Founded in 1976 and covering an area of 108 square kilometres, situated between the town of Sesimbra and the city of Setúbal.

Lagoa do Fogo

Nature reserve at a volcanic crater lake

A crater lake within the Água de Pau Massif stratovolcano in the center of the island of São Miguel in the Portuguese archipelago of the Azores.

Praia da Marinha

Scenic beach amid soaring cliffs

Considered by the Michelin Guide as one of the 10 most beautiful beaches in Europe and as one of the 100 most beautiful beaches in the world.

Serra da Estrela

Glacier, mountain, ski resort, hiking

Is situated in the largest mountain range in Portugal - the Serra da Estrela, and is the source of the rivers Mondego, Zêzere, and Alva.

Oceanário de Lisboa

Waterside aquarium

It is located in the Parque das Nações, which was the exhibition grounds for the Expo '98. It is the largest indoor aquarium in Europe.
